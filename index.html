MVP objective

Digitize and standardize academic mobility and credit homologation in the National Police, reducing processing time, ensuring regulatory consistency, and fostering flexible learning paths.

Speed up evaluations

Automates curriculum comparisons and generates initial recommendations so the committee can focus on the final decision.

Ensure compliance

Includes configurable rules (50% external, 70% internal, course validity, non-homologable subjects) aligned with regulation.

Promote mobility

Enables progression through propedeutic cycles, centralizes equivalences, and supports pathways from technical level to graduate studies.

User profile · DIEPO Academic Committee

Leaders and faculty members responsible for reviewing homologation requests, validating documentation, and ensuring proper application of educational and police regulations.

Key needs

  • Compare programs and syllabi quickly.
  • See automatic alerts on credit limits and course validity.
  • Generate uniform, traceable concepts.
  • Access regulatory references in context.

Usage context

Pre-session reviews, group deliberation with document access, manual adjustments to recommendations, and issuance of final concepts supported by the system.

    Simulated use flow

    Step-by-step representation of the academic committee process using SIMHA.

    1. Request reception: Notification with basic data and attached documents.
    2. Automatic analysis: Course matching, credit calculation, and validity alerts.
    3. Deliberation: Committee manual adjustments and observation logging.
    4. Concept generation: Preliminary document with justifications and applied regulation.
    5. Follow-up: Indicators updated and ability to create new equivalences.

    Technical recommendations

    Guidelines proposed for PMV development and scalability.

    Modular architecture

    Separate frontend, business logic, and database; expose REST API for integrations and potential intranet/SSO deployment.

    Configurable rules

    Admin tables or panel for maximum percentages, validity, exceptions, and credit equivalence (48 hours).

    Security and traceability

    Role-based access control, HTTPS encryption, linked document repository, and immutable audit log.
